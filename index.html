3D Printing 101

Getting Started

Introduction

3D Printing is a really fun hobby that is worth saving up for. You can make a lot of things with 3D prints. You can make mini figures, prototypes, cosplay outfits, and so much more. It is expensive to buy a 3D printer but it is worth saving up for.

Resources

  1. This site will provide basic information on beginner 3D printing: 3D Printing Industry
  2. This site provides a really great guide to pick out your first 3D printer based on price and needs. All3dp
  3. This site is a really good place to get models for free that you can print at home once you have finished getting your printer and setting it up: Thingiverse

Did you know?

3D prints can sometimes be painted to give the figure a more realistic look. This baby yoda was orginally a single color but was painted to make it look exactly like the real Baby Yoda (Grogu).
